Easy Bake Fish

\

Description

  • 3 tablespoons honey
  • 3 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 4 (6 ounce) salmon steaks
  • Â½ teaspoon pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
  2. In a small bowl, mix honey, mustard, and lemon juice. Spread the mixture over the
    salmon steaks. Season with pepper. Arrange in a medium baking dish.
  3. Bake 20 minutes in the preheated oven, or until fish easily flakes with a fork.
